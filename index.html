A mentorship-approach to ensure career success in the next generation of healthcare workers

Medsembly is the premier healthcare network bringing.

students and professionals together under one community.

Guiding you every step of the way

Whether you’re applying to PA school, just coming out of medical school, or you’re looking to make a career change, our mentors are ready to offer you advice and guidance that will advance your careers quickly and set you on the path to success.

Shadowing opportunities and clinical preceptors

Experience the day-in-the-life and what it’s really like working in the medical field. Medsembly offers both in-person and virtual shadowing opportunities and connects you with clinical preceptors in preparation for a rewarding career in healthcare.

Build your healthcare network

Join the Medsembly community and connect with like-minded peers and colleagues from all over the country. Engage in meaningful discussions, private messaging, and more. Your network will be there for you as you progress in your field.

Career insight from experienced professionals

Get first-hand knowledge on your dream career from working professionals. Medsembly gives you the opportunity to get connected with future colleagues as early as freshman year so you can get and stay on the right track to success.

Three Steps to Success on Medsembly

Build Your Profile

The first step to success on Medsembly is a complete profile! Telling us about your experiences and goals helps ensure stronger connections and recommendations. Start with uploading an inviting profile photo, completing your bio, and sharing your experiences. Users with a complete profile have a 80% chance of better recommendations and a greater experience on Medsembly.
